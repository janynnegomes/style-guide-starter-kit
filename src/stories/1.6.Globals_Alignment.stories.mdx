# Alignment

Alignment can occur via absolute positioning, auto margins, elements with inline-block displays and flexbox.

The examples below use mixins and classes to position and center elements relative to each other and their parents.

<div className="subheading">Alignment: Auto Margins: Centering: Horizontal</div>

Use mixins or classes to center elements with margins.

Auto margins are used for centering elements horizontally.

Auto margins are also a useful centering mechanism for elements with fixed widths.

<div className="container bg-gray-mist">
  <div className="center-element  w-300 css-14m39zm h-100 bg-brand-1 text-white spacing" >Horizontally Centered related to the parent</div>
</div>

Absolute positioning is used to center child elements.
